About MARKAZ HADIYA WOMEN'S COLLEGE

Excellence in Every Step

Markaz Hadiya Women's College, Thazhapra, is a premier institution dedicated to the holistic empowerment of women. Affiliated with the world-renowned Jamia Markazu Sakhafathi Ssunniyya, Karanthur, we provide a unique education model that bridges the gap between contemporary academics and profound spiritual wisdom.

Located in the serene landscapes of Thazhapra, our campus offers a sanctuary of knowledge, where students are nurtured with care, safety, and an uncompromising commitment to excellence.

Our Vision

To empower women with a holistic blend of profound moral values and advanced contemporary knowledge, fostering excellence in both spiritual and academic spheres.

Our Mission

To provide a secure and nurturing environment that facilitates high-quality education, character development, and academic excellence, ensuring a safe space for personal and intellectual growth.

Academic Programs

Degree Programs
